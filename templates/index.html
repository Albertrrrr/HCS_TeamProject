What's the shoulder surfing?

Previous Next

The ubiquity of mobile devices brings a lot of benefits to users, but it also exposes them to shoulder surfing.Shoulder surfing – a physical type of social engineering attacks – refers to the act of observing the devicescreen of other people without their permission. Shoulder surfing can occur anywhere where a device isused due to which anyone can be the shoulder surfer, such as strangers, family members, friends, colleagues,or intimate partners.

A social survey on preventive measures for shoulder surfing

This is our questionnaire project description. Please read the project information carefully and then start to participate in the questionnaire.

Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Welcome to our project. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you, as a participant, of your rights and how we handle your data.

Participant Protection

We guarantee that you will not face any risks beyond those common in everyday life during the research process. We have a responsibility to protect you from both physical and mental harm, including, but not limited to, investigations conducted in non-standard environments or those requiring mobility or the use of non-standard equipment.

All experimental materials used are either paper-based or software running on standard hardware, posing no risk associated with non-standard equipment.

Consent and Participation

Your participation is entirely based on explicit consent. We will use your data for the project only after obtaining your explicit consent. If the project results are likely to be used long-term, we will request you sign a written consent form. Otherwise, verbal consent is sufficient and will be explicitly requested at the start of the project.

We have not offered any incentives to participants to ensure your voluntary participation without taking on additional risks for compensation.

We guarantee no information about the evaluation or materials will be intentionally withheld from you. Misleading participants or withholding information is unacceptable.

Age and Capacity

We do not accept participation from individuals under the age of 16 unless parental consent is obtained.

Similarly, for participants who may have impairments that limit their understanding or communication, we will require additional consent.

Rights and Contact

You have the right to withdraw from participation at any time and can contact us whenever necessary. We ensure all participants are aware of this right at the beginning of the study.

We guarantee that neither the researchers nor their supervisors will exert any authority or influence pressure on any participant.

All participants will have the opportunity to discuss and ask questions at the end of the study. We will provide sufficient information to ensure you fully understand the nature of the research.

Data Storage and Security

We commit to storing all data collected from participants in an anonymous form, ensuring your privacy and security.

Special Notice: email

In the context of our questionnaire, we collect your email address and use it as a primary key for the purpose of uniquely identifying participants throughout the study. This practice allows us to accurately track each participant's response, ensuring data integrity and accuracy. Below is a detailed explanation of this practice and how we ensure that this information is not used for any other unauthorised purposes.

Transparency and Purpose

  • Identifying Participants: The sole purpose of using your email address as a primary key is to identify and manage participant data throughout the research process. This approach ensures the accurate attribution of survey responses to the correct participant, while avoiding duplicate submissions.
  • Data Accuracy: Identifying participants through their email addresses allows us to maintain high accuracy and consistency in the survey data, which is crucial for the effectiveness and reliability of the research.

Use Limitations and Consent

  • Limited Use: We commit to using your email address solely for the purpose of the survey. It will not be used for any other activities, including marketing emails or any form of communication without your consent.
  • Obtaining Consent: Before collecting your email address, we will clearly inform you that it will be used as the primary key for the survey, and it will only be collected after obtaining your explicit consent.

Security and Privacy

  • Data Protection: We have implemented robust data protection measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or other forms of improper processing. Your email address and survey data will be securely stored, with access strictly limited to authorized personnel.
  • Rights Protection: We fully respect your privacy and data protection rights. You have the right to know how your data is used and to request the correction or deletion of your personal information at any time.

This Privacy Policy is intended to comprehensively protect your privacy and personal information. We appreciate your participation and trust. If you have any questions or need more information, please contact us through the portal page
